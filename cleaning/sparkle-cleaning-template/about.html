Our Story

SparkleClean was founded on a simple but powerful idea: a clean home is a happy home. We got tired of hearing stories about unreliable cleaners and services that cut corners. We knew there had to be a better way.

We set out to build a company that was different. A company built on trust, reliability, and a genuine passion for cleaning. We invest in our people, using only vetted, insured, and highly-trained professionals. We are committed to using eco-friendly products that are safe for your family, your pets, and our planet.

Our SparkleClean Guarantee

Your happiness is our top priority. If you're not 100% satisfied with our service, we will return and re-clean the affected areas free of charge. It's that simple.
