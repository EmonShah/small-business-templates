Frequently Asked Questions

Are the prices listed final?

The prices shown are base rates for a standard-sized home (e.g., up to 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms). Larger homes or homes requiring extra attention may be subject to additional charges. We provide a firm quote after a brief consultation.

Do you offer recurring service discounts?

Yes! We offer discounts for recurring services. You can save 15% with weekly service, 10% with bi-weekly service, and 5% with monthly service. Contact us to set up a recurring plan.
